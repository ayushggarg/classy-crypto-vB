Letter Number


This is one of the easiest and weakest cipher that can be recognised with no efforts. We replace letters with a number: A=1, B=2, C=3, etc.
When encrypting, only letters will be encoded. Non-letters will be treated like spaces. When decrypting, numbers will be changed back to letters, hyphens will be removed, and the rest fill act like spaces.




How to use the above tool?

To encrypt or decrypt your text, enter your plaintext or ciphertext respectively and press the encrypt or decrypt button accordingly.
