Frequency Analysis



In cryptanalysis, it is the study of of the frequency of letters. In English certain letters like (A,E) occur more more frequently then the other letters like (Q,Z). This tool helps you to decrypt simple substitution cipher and other classical ciphers. ETAOIN SHRDLU represents the 12 most frequent letters in typical English language text.




How to use the above tool?

To compute the frequency of letters, enter the text in the given textbox and hit calculate to show results for the text.
