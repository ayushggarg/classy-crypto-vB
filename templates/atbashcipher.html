Atbash



The Atbash Cipher is a very common, simple and very weak cipher. It was for the Hebrew alphabet, but modified to work with English alphabet as well.

It is a simple monoalphabetic substitution cipher with only one possible key thus making it very weak cipher. However, this may not have been an issue at the time when cipher was first devised.
